A role-based quiz that helps teams inside an organisation get comfortable with GDPR and the EU AI Act. Pick the team you're on, and the questions adjust to the situations that team actually runs into.

What this tool is for

▼

Most organisations rolling out AI hit the same wall: everyone agrees compliance matters, but nobody outside legal has a working sense of what the EU AI Act or GDPR actually requires for their day-to-day decisions. That gap usually gets filled by a lawyer sitting in on every conversation, or by teams guessing and hoping.

This quiz gives non-technical teams a way to build that working knowledge themselves. Instead of one generic set of questions, it's organised by function: HR, Marketing, Sales, Engineering, Legal, and Leadership each get scenarios drawn from the situations they actually run into, alongside the shared fundamentals everyone should know.

Use it as a self-paced onboarding step before an AI rollout, a refresher ahead of a new use case, or a shared reference point in a workshop: a way to get a team's baseline understanding aligned before the harder conversations start.
